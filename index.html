Welcome to your Kurukshetra University Kurukshetra Alumni Association

We see this Kurukshetra University Kurukshetra Alumni Association as a circle where we hold each other and help each other reach goals. We take the support that we need and offer what we can. We see ourselves as complete only when we are able to do this together and for each other. This vision for this community is to create an ecosystem of support for all alumni to fulfill their life goals. Our goals include making all alumni independent by supporting them through education, building a sense of community and creating interest based pathways. What we hope this portal does is to give each of you a space to fearlessly share your voice, be there for each other and find ways to give back to your Kurukshetra University Kurukshetra Alumni Association.
